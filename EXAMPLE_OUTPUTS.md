## Example 4: When is the Premises delivery date?

```markdown
## When is the Premises delivery date?

**May 1, 2020**

---

## Source & Evidence

- **Document Section(s):** Section 2.2 (Delivery of Premises), Section 3.1 (Tenant Work)
- **Page(s):** Page 3, Page 5
- **Exact Contract Language:**
  > "Landlord shall deliver possession of the Premises to Tenant on or before 
  > May 1, 2020 (the 'Delivery Date'), in broom-clean condition and free of 
  > all tenants and occupants."

---

## Interpretation Notes

The Premises delivery date was established as May 1, 2020, which is ten (10) 
days prior to the Lease Commencement Date. This early delivery period allows 
Tenant to complete necessary tenant improvements and preparations before the 
lease term officially begins and rent obligations commence.

---

## Confidence

**High**

- Reason: Explicitly stated with specific date in the Delivery of Premises section.

---

## Caveats & Exceptions

The actual delivery is subject to completion of landlord's work as specified 
in Section 3.0.

- **Review Required:** No
```

## Example 5: What is the size of the Premises?

```markdown
## What is the size of the Premises?

**Approximately 3,200 square feet**

---

## Source & Evidence

- **Document Section(s):** Section 1.2 (Premises), Exhibit A (Floor Plan)
- **Page(s):** Page 1, Exhibit A
- **Exact Contract Language:**
  > "The Premises consist of approximately three thousand two hundred (3,200) 
  > rentable square feet located on the ground floor of the Building, as more 
  > particularly described in Exhibit A attached hereto."

---

## Interpretation Notes

The square footage is stated as 'approximately' 3,200 square feet, indicating 
it is a good-faith estimate subject to standard measurement practices. The exact 
boundaries are defined in Exhibit A floor plan. This measurement follows the BOMA 
(Building Owners and Managers Association) standard for rentable square footage.

---

## Confidence

**High**

- Reason: Explicitly stated in the Premises definition section with cross-reference 
  to detailed floor plan.

---

## Caveats & Exceptions

The term 'approximately' indicates the actual size may vary slightly from 3,200 
square feet upon final measurement. Standard lease practice allows for minor 
variations (+/- 2-3%) in stated square footage.

---

## Traceability Metadata

- **Document ID:** Chef_Art_Smith_Lease_May_11_2020
- **Definition Type:** Explicit
- **Review Required:** No

Citations:
- Chef Art Smith Lease - Executed May 11, 2020
- Section 1.2 (Premises), Exhibit A (Floor Plan) - Page 1, Exhibit A
```
